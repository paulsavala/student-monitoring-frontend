Getting started

Getting setup is easy, just follow these steps:

Login to Canvas

Start by logging into Canvas

Go to Account settings

Click on "Account" in the top-left corner, then click "Settings"

Approved integrations

Scroll down until you see "Approved Integrations", then click "New Access Token"

Generate an access token

For "Purpose" type "Student monitoring" (or whatever name you would like to give it, the name is just for your reference). Leave "Expires" blank.

Copy the token

Copy the value show in "Token". This is the "API Key" you will be asked for when registering with Student Monitoring.

Register with Student Monitoring

Now you're ready to get registered. Click on "Login/Register" in the top-right corner of this page. Or, click on this link.

Login with your @stedwards.edu account

You will be redirected to login using Google. Just click on your St. Edward's email address.

Complete your registration

Type in your name and select your department. Then, in the "API Token" field, paste the "Access Token" you just copied from Canvas. Click "Register" and you're done!
